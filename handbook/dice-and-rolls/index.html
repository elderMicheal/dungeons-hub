How d20 rolls, advantage, disadvantage, damage dice, and checks usually work.

The d20

The d20 is used for many uncertain outcomes. Roll it, add the relevant bonus from your character sheet, and compare the total to a target number or a creature's armor class.

Advantage and disadvantage

Advantage means roll two d20s and use the higher result. Disadvantage means roll two d20s and use the lower result.

Other dice

d4, d6, d8, d10, and d12 are often used for damage, healing, random tables, or class features.
