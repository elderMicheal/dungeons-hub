What players usually do before, during, and after a game session.

Typical session flow

  1. People connect and settle in.
  2. The DM recaps where things stand.
  3. Players ask questions and choose what to do.
  4. The group moves through roleplay, exploration, problem solving, or combat.
  5. The session ends with notes, rewards, or next steps.

What to do when confused

Ask the DM what your character can tell from the situation. Then describe what your character wants to try. You do not need the correct rule name to participate.
