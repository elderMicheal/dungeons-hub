A library, not a course

The Apprentice Handbook is a public D&D reference for players. It is written for people who may know nothing yet, but it does not require anyone to move through a lesson track.

Use it when a term, tool, rule idea, or table expectation gets confusing.

Depth labels

Beginner first concepts. Intermediate connected systems. Expert edge cases, variations, and deeper terminology.
